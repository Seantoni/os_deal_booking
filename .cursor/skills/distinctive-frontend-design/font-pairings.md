## Usage Tips

- **Variable fonts**: Prefer variable weight fonts (e.g., Outfit, Sora) for fine-tuned control
- **Loading**: Use `font-display: swap` to avoid FOIT
- **Scale**: Headings at 2-4x body size minimum for display fonts to shine
- **Weight contrast**: Pair heavy display weights (700-900) with regular body (400)
- **Spacing**: Display fonts often benefit from negative `letter-spacing`; body fonts from slightly positive
